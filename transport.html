Public Transport

Dublin Bikes

Dublin Bikes is an eco-friendly, convenient, and affordable way to explore the city.

How It Works:

Over 100 docking stations located across the city.

First 30 minutes of every trip is free.

Pricing:

€3.5 for a day pass.

€5 for a 3-day pass.
